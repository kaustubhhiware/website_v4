Social Computing, NLP

Aug 2017 - Present

Leveraging Twitter to detect emergency situations

Research project under Computer Science Department, IIT Kharagpur
Tags : Research, Social Computing, NLP

The objective is to create a pre-warning system to detect potential disaster situations, by contnually monitoring tweets in real-time. This tool would also help us understand the geospatial distribution of important tweets across time. The system can be viewed at savitr.herokuapp.com.
A paper has been accepted for this project in Social Media for Emergency and Response Preparedness (SMERP) workshop at Webconf (WWW'18) conference. and a poster at International Conference of Networked Digital Earth (ICDNE)
